technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai. It is a part of the Tata Group
and operates in 150 locations across 46 countries. In March 2024, it was reported that TCS had more than
601,546 employees worldwide.


In 1975, TCS delivered an electronic depository and trading system called SECOM for Swiss company SIS SegaInterSettle;
it also developed System X for the Canadian Depository System and automated the Johannesburg Stock Exchange.[26] TCS
associated with a Swiss partner, TKS Teknosoft, which it later acquired.[27]In 1980, TCS established India's first dedicated
